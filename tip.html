Wellness Tips

Practical, bite-sized tips to calm your mind and build daily habits

Mindful Breathing

Take 5 slow breaths: inhale 4s, hold 2s, exhale 6s. Repeat 5 times to reset stress.

Short Walks

Walk for 10 minutes outdoors — movement + fresh air improves mood and focus.

Prioritize Sleep

Keep a consistent bedtime; aim for 7–8 hours. Good sleep = better emotional regulation.

Hydrate

Drink water regularly — dehydration can mimic fatigue and low mood.

Journaling

Write 3 things you’re grateful for every day — builds positivity habitually.

Calm Sounds

Use 10 minutes of calming music or nature sounds before bed or during breaks.
