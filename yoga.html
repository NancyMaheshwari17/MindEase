Calm Yoga Flows

Gentle routines to relax your mind and body easily.

Morning Relax Flow (5 min)

A calm warm-up for fresh mornings.

  • 3 deep breaths
  • Neck rolls — 5 each side
  • Cat–Cow stretch — 8 rounds
  • Child’s pose — 30–60 sec

Desk Break Stretch (3–5 min)

Perfect for study/work breaks.

  • Shoulder rolls — 10 each
  • Seated twist — 20 sec each
  • Wrist stretch — 20 sec
  • Sitting forward fold

Bedtime Cool Down (7 min)

Slow movements to help relax before sleep.

  • Legs up the wall — 2–3 min
  • Supine twist — 45 sec
  • Knees-to-chest — 1 min
  • Savasana — 1 min

Breathing Reset (1–2 min)

Instant calm for stressful moments.

  • Inhale 4 sec
  • Hold 2 sec
  • Exhale 6 sec
  • Repeat 6–8 times

Remember: Yoga should never feel painful. Move slowly, breathe deeply, and stay relaxed.
